The Kitty Norville Series

Plot Summary

The Kitty Norville series is urban fantasy focusing on a world where the supernatural is real, and is outed on midnight talk radio. Once the main charater, Kitty, admits to being a werewolf on live radio public reaction is mixed. There are those who think is just a publicity stunt, and there are those who believe it is real.

Kitty became a werewolf in her last year of college. She has been hiding that fact from everyone. As a late night DJ one night she gets bored with playing old rock and roll and starts discussing the supernatural with her listeners. Ratings are through the roof and her new show The Midnight Hour is born.
